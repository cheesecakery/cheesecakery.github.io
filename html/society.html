Joining the Sir Cheesecake Steak™ society

If one would like to join the Sir Cheesecake Steak society there are a number of points one must be clearsighted on.

Firstly, it is imperative that you have scored highly on the quiz. Of course, the only way you should be able to access this page is if you have scored highly -- so I presume this point is rendered obsolete. If you have not scored highly, I highly suggest you tab off this page. There is information here only for the brightest of eyes.

Secondly, one must have an inbred -- I mean ingrown -- adoration for the values of our society: secrecy, a susceptibility to cult-like behaviour, and most importantly, a love for cheesecake. Not the cheesecake itself, but what the round dessert represents, you know? I think you know. Let me know if you don't know, but I presume you do know. Because, you know.

Benefits of membership

Of course, it must be said that there are a multitude of benefits to joining our society.

Such benefits include, but are not limited to:

  • 20% off at the Cheesecake Factory (& access to the 'secret' menu).
  • A subscription to the society's dearest magazine, the Cheesecake and Early Modern Period and Wine Club Bulletin.
  • Access to the SCS library in order to research propaganda relating to HVII, the king supreme.
  • Monthly zoom calls with the rest of the society membership in order to discuss various imperial matters and form life-long bonds with fellows over the internet. It is ever so important in this day and age to strengthen our bonds; we are running out of number.
  • Something to be proud of.
  • A passion.
  • A commitment...!
  • Aren't we all so lonely these days? What is the harm in a little money spent.

The cost

Can't we discuss this later? We've drilled on about the benefits and such -- really, if you want to be a part of this society, what you must understand is that money is no object. Repeat after me - money is no object.

But if you really must know, membership costs £50 each month. We are currently doing a special: a year round deal for the sweet price of £700. In today's economy, that's a steal!
