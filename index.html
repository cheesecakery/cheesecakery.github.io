Where my journey on this splendid earth didst begin -- About the man behind the myth. Find what you seek here!

I was once a King. I hope that is clear once one peers upon my supple fingers and velvet-born hat. I am not a King anymore. I had to retire. To look after the children and the wife, you know. Being a King is a very stressful job. Power is a stressful concept. I don't think we are really built to deal with power. That's why a lot of us crumble. You either lose your morals or your mind. Or really, I suppose, you lose both. My predecessor - Richard III. He killed his nephews. Sorry, allegedly killed his nephews. That is what power does to a man's mind.

Where I reside now

Now, I am Sir Cheesecake Steak. Whilst no longer adorning the title of King, I feel much more proud of my current title. It feels as though, for once, I can let free the chains of others' expectations and finally feel free within my own skin. No pressures like petitions or the like. No constant eye peering over my shoulder -- for my safety, no doubt, but none the less an eyesore -- and no more need for majestic appearance. I usurped the kingdom through slaying Richard III, himself a feared usurper. And in my resignation, I hope to put this whole 'usurpation' mess to bed.
